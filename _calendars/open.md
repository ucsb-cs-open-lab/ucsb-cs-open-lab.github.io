This calendar shows the times that the open lab rooms are available for scheduling office hours. It should show the availability in rooms 2510, 3525 and 3536.  
* You may schedule during any block that shows "Open Lab Hours"
* Do not schedule during a scheduled course.

If you are scheduling outside of these blocks, please use another space such as the TA trailer, or Zoom.
